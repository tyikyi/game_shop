Why do we play games?

Game is life, life is game! If game has actions, then life has it's inverse reactions! Game shows us the way to go in! We start to go, we feel no worry as it seems too easy to go forward! But all on a sudden, enemies come! Then we fear, we cry, we lag behind and feel so alone! We look backward to check if we have come in the right direction! We think, we are so close to death! Nevertheless, we try our best! We try till the last beep sound of our heart beats! And eventually, we win! This is the reason why we do play games!
